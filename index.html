Railroad Ink Assistant

By Jack Chen

Railroad Ink Assistant is your essential companion, enhancing your gaming experience by serving as a portable database. Seamlessly integrated into gameplay, it automates tasks from dice rolling to quick rule references. All you need is your game boards and markers.

Railroad Ink Assistant also includes numerous houserules of select expansions, designed by the creator of Railroad Ink Assistant to enhance gameplay. These can be optionally enabled if you are willing experiment beyond what the designer intended.
